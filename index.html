Our Story

Modern Costa Rican Street Food

At Soda Stereo Restaurante we reinterpret Costa Rican cuisine with a modern, professional touch — sourcing fresh ingredients from local farming families and producers in the area whenever possible.

Located in the heart of La Fortuna, we work with passion so every guest leaves happy and eager to return. From the classic casado to grilled cuts, fresh pasta and the best Birria tacos in the area — all served in an atmosphere of good music, warmth and Pura Vida.
